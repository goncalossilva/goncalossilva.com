Gonçalo Silva

I’m Gonçalo, CTO at Doist, the bootstrapped and globally distributed bunch behind Todoist, Twist, and more (soon!).

Before joining Doist, I founded Heavy Player with João. We wanted to build mobile games with delightful stories and mechanics, but we also needed to pay rent. That’s how I met Amir, who was trying to get Todoist (a side project at the time) off the ground through freelancers alone.

Freelancing wasn’t new to me. I’d worked with clients such as Shopify, Engine Yard, and PT Inovação. Earlier still, I joined Tecla Colorida, where I worked as an engineer and later as a sysadmin on Escolinhas, a platform for primary school children, teachers, parents, and, let’s be honest, government contracts.

I’ve contributed to open source throughout my career. If you’ve used software built with Ruby, Rails, Kotlin, Android, or GNOME, there’s a decent chance you’ve run code I wrote.

When not working, I’m usually enjoying my three little rascals.
